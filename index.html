About CPA (Tanzania)

Certified Public Accountant (CPA) is a professional accounting qualification offered under the National Board of Accountants and Auditors (NBAA) in Tanzania. The course equips students with knowledge in accounting, auditing, taxation, finance, and management.

CPA Levels

  • Foundation Level – Basic accounting and business concepts
  • Intermediate Level – Financial reporting, auditing, taxation
  • Final Level – Advanced accounting, strategic management, public finance
